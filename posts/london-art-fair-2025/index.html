spoke at london art fair ⏳

Posted on Jan 26, 2025
tl;dr: did an interview about Poor Artists and then ran back to liverpool

I did a flying visit to London Art Fair to speak about our book Poor Artists. We were in conversation with Verity Babbs who was precise and funny and good at turning the book inside-out for anyone in the audience who hadn’t read it yet. BookBar were outside the door selling Poor Artists, and BookBar were great as well. And then I came home, ill, and I wrote this text about the experience of visiting the world of extreme wealth for one weird afternoon.
