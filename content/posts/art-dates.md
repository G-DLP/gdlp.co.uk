I've started a new podcast called [Art Dates](https://artdates.co.uk) 

I'm going to be interviewing people as we walk around exhibitions. I'll have my camera with me, so episodes will take the form of audio plus a bunch of film photos, and the transcription if you want it. I don't wanna mess with video for now. I love film so much.

[Take It Easy](https://takeiteasylab.com/) are supporting the development which is great!

There's no set schedule. This is for fun, for free will, for making friends, for making the most of my new stamina. I'll be recording the next episode the day after tomorrow when I'm in Ljubljana : ) I am going to be recording a bunch of episodes when I'm in Chile later this year.
